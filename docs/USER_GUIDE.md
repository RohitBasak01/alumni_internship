# Alumni Network Platform - User Guide

## Introduction

Welcome to the Alumni Network Platform! This guide will help you navigate and make the most of your alumni community platform. Whether you're reconnecting with old classmates, finding mentorship opportunities, or exploring career resources, this guide covers everything you need to know.

## Getting Started

### 1. Registration and Login

- **New Users**: Visit your institution's alumni portal and click "Register" to create an account
- **Email Verification**: Check your email for a verification link after registration
- **Password Setup**: Create a secure password during the registration process
- **Login**: Use your registered email and password to access the platform

### 2. Profile Setup

Complete your profile to get the most out of the platform:

- **Basic Information**: Name, graduation year, degree/department
- **Career Details**: Current company, position, industry
- **Skills & Interests**: Add relevant skills to help others find you
- **Profile Picture**: Upload a professional photo
- **Contact Preferences**: Choose what information is visible to others

## Platform Features

### Dashboard

Your personalized dashboard shows:

- Recent activity from your network
- Upcoming events
- New job postings
- Connection requests
- Notifications

### Alumni Directory

- **Search**: Find alumni by name, graduation year, company, or skills
- **Filter**: Narrow results by location, industry, or current role
- **Connect**: Send connection requests to build your network
- **View Profiles**: See detailed profiles with career history and contact information

### Feed & Community

- **Post Updates**: Share news, achievements, or questions with the community
- **Like & Comment**: Engage with posts from other alumni
- **Media Sharing**: Upload photos and documents (where enabled)
- **Hashtags**: Use relevant tags to categorize your posts

### Events

- **Browse Events**: View upcoming alumni events, reunions, and webinars
- **Register**: RSVP for events you plan to attend
- **Create Events**: Organizers can create new events (admin permission required)
- **Calendar Integration**: Add events to your personal calendar

### Jobs & Career

- **Job Board**: Browse job postings from alumni and partner companies
- **Apply**: Submit applications directly through the platform
- **Post Jobs**: Companies can post openings (admin approval required)
- **Career Resources**: Access career development materials and tips

### Mentorship

- **Find Mentors**: Search for alumni willing to mentor others
- **Request Mentorship**: Send mentorship requests to experienced alumni
- **Chat**: Communicate with mentors through secure messaging
- **Schedule Calls**: Arrange virtual meetings through the platform

### Groups

- **Join Communities**: Participate in special interest groups (by industry, location, etc.)
- **Group Discussions**: Engage in topic-specific conversations
- **Share Resources**: Upload documents and links relevant to the group
- **Event Coordination**: Plan group-specific meetups and activities

### Business Directory

- **Browse Businesses**: Discover companies founded or led by alumni
- **Add Your Business**: List your company in the directory
- **Network**: Connect with alumni entrepreneurs and business owners

## Mobile Experience

The platform is fully responsive and works on:

- **Smartphones**: All features available with touch-friendly interface
- **Tablets**: Optimized for larger touch screens
- **Desktop**: Full-featured experience with additional navigation options

### Mobile-Specific Features

- **Hamburger Menu**: Access navigation from the top-left menu icon
- **Touch Gestures**: Swipe to navigate, tap to interact
- **Mobile Notifications**: Receive push notifications for important updates
- **Camera Integration**: Upload photos directly from your device

## Privacy & Security

### Your Data

- **Control Visibility**: Choose what information is public vs. private
- **Connection Privacy**: Only connected alumni see your full contact details
- **Data Export**: Request a copy of your data at any time
- **Account Deletion**: You can delete your account and all associated data

### Safety Features

- **Report Content**: Flag inappropriate posts or behavior
- **Block Users**: Prevent specific users from contacting you
- **Privacy Settings**: Customize who can see your activity and profile
- **Secure Messaging**: All direct messages are encrypted

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Login Problems

- **Forgot Password**: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
- **Account Locked**: Contact your institution's admin if locked out
- **Email Not Received**: Check spam folder; request a new verification email

#### Technical Issues

- **Page Not Loading**: Clear browser cache or try a different browser
- **Upload Failures**: Ensure files are under 10MB and in supported formats
- **Slow Performance**: Check your internet connection; the platform works best with modern browsers

#### Feature-Specific Help

- **Can't Find Someone**: Try different search terms or ask an admin for help
- **Event Registration Issues**: Contact the event organizer
- **Messaging Problems**: Ensure you're connected to the person you're trying to message

### Getting Help

- **Platform Support**: Email support@your-institution.edu
- **Technical Issues**: Contact IT support at your institution
- **Feature Requests**: Suggest improvements through the feedback form
- **Bug Reports**: Use the "Report a Problem" link in the footer

## Best Practices

### Networking Etiquette

- **Personalize Connection Requests**: Mention how you know the person or why you'd like to connect
- **Be Professional**: Maintain a respectful tone in all communications
- **Give Before You Ask**: Offer help or insights before requesting assistance
- **Follow Up**: Thank people for their time and advice

### Profile Optimization

- **Complete All Sections**: A complete profile gets 10x more views
- **Use Professional Photos**: Clear, recent headshots work best
- **Update Regularly**: Keep your current position and skills up to date
- **Add Keywords**: Include industry terms to improve searchability

### Community Engagement

- **Participate Regularly**: Visit at least once a week
- **Share Valuable Content**: Post industry news, job opportunities, or helpful resources
- **Respond Promptly**: Answer messages and connection requests within 48 hours
- **Celebrate Others**: Congratulate alumni on achievements and milestones

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Who can see my profile information?

A: By default, basic profile information (name, graduation year, current company/position) is visible to all logged-in alumni from your institution. You can adjust privacy settings in your account.

### Q: How do I reset my password?

A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

### Q: Can I use the platform on my phone?

A: Yes! The platform is fully mobile-responsive. You can access all features through your mobile browser.

### Q: How do I report inappropriate content?

A: Click the three-dot menu on any post and select "Report". Administrators will review and take appropriate action.

### Q: Is there a mobile app?

A: Currently, the platform is web-based but works perfectly in mobile browsers. A native mobile app may be developed in the future.

### Q: How do I delete my account?

A: Contact your institution's platform administrator to request account deletion.
